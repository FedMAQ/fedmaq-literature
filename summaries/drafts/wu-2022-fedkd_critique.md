- The statement in the Limitations section that FedKD “roughly doubling local computation costs compared to standard FL” is incorrect. The paper explicitly states that FedKD “will slightly increase the computational cost” (because the mentee model is much smaller than the mentor), not double it. This should be corrected to reflect the paper’s own description.
- The summary’s description of the server aggregation step says the server computes the average \(g = \frac{1}{N}\sum_i g_i\) and then distributes it without further client-side division. In the paper’s algorithm, however, the server aggregates the sum \(g = \sum_i g_i\) and the client later divides by \(N\) when updating the mentee (\(\Theta^s \leftarrow \Theta^s - \eta_s g / N\)). The summary should either present the algorithm exactly as in the paper or clarify that the effective update uses the average but the actual division step occurs on the client side.
