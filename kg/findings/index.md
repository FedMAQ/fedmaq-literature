# Findings

`type: Finding` documents that synthesize evidence **across** papers into claims
relevant to the FedMAQ manuscript — each finding stating a claim, the papers that
support (or contradict) it, and the key evidence.

_Scaffolded; not yet populated._ This layer is Phase 3 of the OKF restructure and
replaces the old `syntheses/` drafts. Raw material already lives in the
`## 5. FedMAQ Thesis Relevance` sections of the [paper nodes](/papers/); findings
will lift and consolidate those into standalone, citable claims (e.g. "adaptive
bit-width outperforms uniform quantization under client heterogeneity") with links
to the supporting [papers](/papers/) and [concepts](/concepts/).
